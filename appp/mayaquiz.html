Aurora Adventure

Welcome, Space Explorer! Get ready for a journey to discover the magical lights in the sky and the amazing space weather that creates them.

Our Star, the Sun!

Our adventure starts with the Sun. It's not just a big ball of light! It constantly sends out a stream of tiny particles called the solar wind. Sometimes, it has big bursts of energy called solar flares!

Earth's Force Field!

Luckily, Earth has a special, invisible shield called the magnetosphere. It protects us from most of the solar wind, like a superhero's force field!

The Sky Dancers!

Some solar wind particles get sneak past the shield near the North and South Poles. They zip down and bump into gases in our air, making them glow! These beautiful glowing lights are the Aurora!
